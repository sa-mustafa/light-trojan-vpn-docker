History.

The VSG was initially developed for the International Institute of Earthquake Engineering and Seismology, Tehran, Iran. The software requires special lighting and controlled condition for optimal image capture. The software uses optical flow methods for computation of motion between consecutive image frames. This motion is then converted to strain and displacement fields in X and Y directions. The output is validated using experimental and numerical methods. Using a procedure called calibration, the camera can make metric measurements. The most important step in this technology is take suitable images during the process.
